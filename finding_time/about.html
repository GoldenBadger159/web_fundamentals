Many friend groups struggle against a common enemy: scheduling. Our founders dealt with all the same struggles in collaboration between study groups, scheduling game nights, and even finding time to spend with the in-laws.

Scheduling fun activities became a major stressor rather than the beginnings of a great time with loved ones. That's why we came up with the idea of Finding Time; To make that process far less painful for our users.

We offer a unique perspective on comparing calendars with many options for anonymity which may decrease unneccesary animosity between members. Using our software will grant flexibility and consistency to friend groups all over the world.

But what makes us different from other companies? Our unique approach to scheduling is founded on the pricipal that human interaction is necessary to your well-being. Each memeber of our team believes in our "people first" mentality. With us you can garauntee that you are valued and that your scheduling needs will be met.
